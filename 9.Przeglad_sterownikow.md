# GE Fanuc

## SERIES 90-30

![Photo of the series 90-30,width=300](http://przemyslowa-automatyka.com.pl/media/catalog/category/ge9030.jpg)

Seria 90-30 to seria sterowników PLC, których największą zaletą jest łatwość obsługi oraz instalacji. Są to sterowniki średniej wielkości, ale już skala i możliwości zastosowań tych urządzeń są duże. Warto wspomnieć między innymi o dużej bazie dodatkowych funkcji, które umożliwiają np., instalowanie specjalistycznego oprogramowania. Zarówno automatyzacja pojedynczych urządzeń przemysłowych oraz maszyn, jak również kompletna automatyzacja linii produkcyjnych, z urządzeniami z tej serii nie jest żadnym problemem.

## SERIES 6

![Photo of the series 6,width=300](http://przemyslowa-automatyka.com.pl/media/catalog/category/ge6.jpg)

Sterownik series 6 został wyprodukowany przez firmę GE Fanuc w 1980 roku. Jest to pierwszy w całości modułowy sterownik PLC produkowany przez tę firmę. Do dnia dzisiejszego liczba zastosowań sterowników PLC z serii 6 przekracza pół miliona na całym świecie. Trudno zliczyć i wymienić rodzaje maszyn produkcyjnych oraz linii, które zostały wyposażone w ten niezwykle efektywny sterownik, który do dnia dzisiejszego idealnie sprawdza się w różnego rodzaju urządzeniach z zakresu automatyki przemysłowej. Na uwagę zasługuje przede wszystkim moduł pamięci, a także moduły komunikacyjne, które odpowiadają za przesyłanie danych.

## PACSYSTEMS RX3I


![Photo of the RX3i,width=300](http://przemyslowa-automatyka.com.pl/media/catalog/category/pac_rx3i.jpg)

Seria RX3I sterowników PLC od firmy GE Fanuc to niezbędny i najnowszy dodatek do systemów PAC. Jeden silnik sterowania, który posiada niniejszy sterownik odpowiada za wysoka wydajność, a szybki procesor to gwarancja wysokiej przepustowości, bez tworzenia się tzw. gardeł informacyjnych. Warto zwrócić uwagę na zastosowanie w tym modelu płyty montażowej i uniwersalnej magistrali PCI, co w znacznym stopniu optymalizuje działanie urządzenia. Tego typu rozwiązania firma GE Fanuc stosuje jako jedna z pierwszych, co w znacznym stopniu powoduje, że sterowniki PLC tej firmy są tak nowoczesne.

## PACSYSTEMS RX7I

![Photo of the VersaMax Nano,width=300](http://przemyslowa-automatyka.com.pl/media/catalog/category/pacrx7.jpg)

Seria RX7I systemu PLC to kolejna wersja sterowników dla systemu PAC. Tak samo jak sterowniki RX3I, wyposażone są one w uniwersalne środowisko programowania, co gwarantuje mobilność aplikacji na różnego rodzaju platformach sprzętowych. Urządzenie jest wyposażone w dwa porty szeregowe, jeden port RS-232, jeden port RS-235 oraz porty Ethernet. Dzięki swojej konstrukcji, urządzenie może pracować w różnych środowiskach sieciowych. Pamięć użytkownika zastosowana w sterownikach tej serii może wynosić maksymalnie 10 megabajtów.

## SERIES 1

![Photo of the VersaMax Nano,width=300](http://przemyslowa-automatyka.com.pl/media/catalog/category/ge1.JPG)

Sterowniki z serii 1 produkowane przez firmę GE Fanuc, cechują się niezawodnością, szybkością działania i wieloma możliwościami konfiguracji urządzenia, w zależności od  potrzeb. To jeden z pierwszych na rynku, modułowych sterowników PLC do komputerów programowalnych. Jego niewielki rozmiar, nieprzekraczający wielkością pudełka po butach, to dodatkowy atut urządzenia. Pamięć operacyjna i niezwykle wydajny procesor sprawiają, że to niewielkie urządzenie zdolne jest do potężnych obliczeń, a w dodatku gwarantuje sprawną, szybką i ekonomiczną pracę. Bez względu na to do jakich zastosowań będzie przeznaczony sterownik z serii 1, można być pewnym, że dzięki swoim właściwościom, sprawdzi się doskonale.

## DURUS PLC

![Photo of the VersaMax Nano,width=300](http://przemyslowa-automatyka.com.pl/media/catalog/category/durus.jpg)

Jeśli potrzebujemy programowalnego komputera logicznego do obsługi niewielkich i niezbyt skomplikowanych aplikacji z dziedziny automatyki przemysłowej, model Durus PLC to zdecydowanie najlepsza i najbardziej efektywna propozycja od firmy GE Fanuc. Urządzenie zostało zbudowane z myślą o wszystkich tych, którzy cenią sobie prostotę i łatwość w programowaniu, a także szybka obsługę urządzeń automatyki przemysłowej. Bez dwóch zdań, wielką zaletą tego systemu jest szybki czas rozruchu, co w efektywny sposób obniża koszty i zapewnia szybką i stabilną prace całego systemu.

# VersaMax Nano

![Photo of the VersaMax Nano,width=300](https://www.astor.com.pl/images/Produkty/Sterowanie/Sterowniki_PLC/VersaMax_Nano/versamax_nano.png)

### VersaMax Nano PLCs 

Efficient programmable logic controllers designed to meet the needs of smaller packaging machines, dispensing machines and applications requiring relay replacement (up to 6 inputs and 4 outputs). Most often used for the simplest control systems in which there is a small, limited number of supported control signals. Easy configuration and quick startup.Occurs in:

- devices performing repetitive, simple sequences  
- distributed installations as data collection systems 

### In distributed systems  

The built-in serial port allows you to connect a radio modem or GSM modem (devices enabling remote communication and sending collected data to superior control systems) 

### Communication  

The RS232 serial port built into the VersaMax Nano controller enables operation of simple devices such as scanners, scales or magnetic card readers using the Series I/O protocol. 

### Simple programming and setting 

The smallest family of GE Intelligent Platforms controllers is programmed using Proficy Machine Edition in LAD or Derived List format with functions known from Control class controllers. 

- advanced programming features such as a built-in high-speed counter function,  

- Support for mathematical functions, floating-point operations, procedures, and a general PID controller allows you to learn programming, which will later be made available to administrators. 

### Built-in signal handling 

The Nano series has built-in support for discrete I/O signals and can be equipped with one analog voltage input. Input and channel diagnostics are provided by means of connected LED drivers, which facilitates and speeds up the service of devices. 

#### VersaMax Nano PLCs feature: 

▪ Two non-removable recessed “box-style” terminal strips  

▪ An external Run/Stop switch can be wired to the Nano PLC. The switch can be configured as a run/stop switch, or a memory protect switch, and used for clearing faults when a fatal fault exists  

▪ Configurable to read configuration at power-up from either RAM or flash memory (ROM). Can also be configured to read application program from flash at power-up  

▪ Capacitor backs up RAM for at least 30 minutes on hardware revisions C or above 

▪ Full-featured programming Instruction Set with floating point math 

▪ 2K words of program memory, 256 words of registers 

▪ built-in RS232 serial port  

▪ local diagnostics using LEDs  

▪ built-in 10 kHz high-speed counter input  

▪ free Proficy Machine Edition utility software 


# VersaMax Micro

![Photo of the controller,width=300](https://www.astor.com.pl/images/Produkty/Sterowanie/Sterowniki_PLC/VersaMax_Micro/VersaMax-hr_1100.jpg)


### VersaMax Micro Series PLC Controllers: Empowering Your Control Systems

Unlock the potential of your control systems with the VersaMax Micro Series PLC controllers by Emerson Industrial Automation & Controls. These controllers, available in 26 distinct models, are tailor-made for small to medium-sized control systems. Offering a compact design and a wealth of features, they seamlessly communicate with automation systems and efficiently handle I/O signals, ranging from just a few to an impressive 320.

#### Key Features:

**1. Expandability Beyond Limits:**
   - Scale your control system with ease by adding up to four expansion modules.
   - Accommodate a maximum of 320 input and output signals, ensuring flexibility for diverse applications.

**2. Seamless Connectivity:**
   - Equipped with an integrated RS232 serial port for easy connectivity.
   - Optional modules available for RS232, RS485, Ethernet, and USB connections, enabling versatile communication options.

**3. Specialized Inputs/Outputs:**
   - Incorporates 4 built-in High-Speed Counter (HSC) inputs capable of handling frequencies up to 100kHz.
   - Features 1 Pulse Width Modulation (PWM) output with a 65kHz capability.
   - Supports 32-bit counters for precise counting tasks.

**4. Analog Proficiency:**
   - Employs 12-bit analog converters for accurate analog signal management.
   - Ideal for applications requiring measurement of current, voltage, thermocouples, and resistive sensors.

**5. Motion Control Capabilities:**
   - Enables motion programming for control over various machinery.
   - Supports integration with up to 4 VersaMotion servo drives for enhanced control.

**6. Program Flexibility:**
   - Some models offer 48kB of RAM and Flash memory, allowing the creation of intricate control programs.
   - Convenient programming with Machine Edition Lite or Professional Suite, including the ability to upload programs on the fly.

**7. Continuous Operation:**
   - All VersaMax Micro PLCs feature both RAM and Flash memory.
   - Micro Plus models with 48kB of dedicated program memory allow updates without disrupting operation.

**8. Versatile Communication:**
   - Multiple built-in communication ports include RS232, RS485, Ethernet, and USB.
   - Compatible with various communication protocols such as Modbus RTU, Modbus TCP, SNPX, SRTP, and more, ensuring seamless integration.

**9. Efficient Servicing:**
   - Streamlined design for swift disconnection of connection terminals, expediting servicing.
   - Optional Flash memory module for program updates without relying on a PC, reducing downtime and costs.

**10. Integration and Compatibility:**
   - Dual independent communication ports support integration with automation solutions from different manufacturers.
   - Capable of servo drive control with transistor outputs for versatile applications.

**11. Diverse Industrial Applications:**
   - VersaMax Micro controllers excel in factory automation, machinery control, process monitoring, HVAC systems, and more.

### Flexible and Scalable Solutions:

VersaMax Micro PLCs offer unmatched flexibility and scalability. Whether you need to expand I/O capabilities with additional modules or use them as standalone controllers for smaller machinery, these controllers provide tailored solutions to meet your specific requirements and budget.

### Compact Powerhouses:

Despite their small footprint, VersaMax Micro PLCs pack a punch with support for up to 64 I/O points (expandable to 176), rapid cycle times, an extensive instruction set, and ample memory for programming versatility.

### Communication Options Galore:

All units feature an RS-232 port, suitable for SNP slave, Modbus RTU, or serial I/O commands. Some models go a step further with an RS-485 port and an additional option port (RS-232, RS-485, or USB). For Ethernet networking, the VersaMax SE (Serial to Ethernet module) provides a seamless solution.

### Boosting Productivity:

The VersaMax Micro family combines the power and performance of customized solutions with the convenience and cost-effectiveness of off-the-shelf technology. With features like high-speed control channels, independent pulse and direction outputs, and configurable I/O, these controllers enhance machine performance while saving time and costs on configurations.

# RX3i
![Photo of the rx3i,width=300](https://automatykab2b.pl/images/3/8/1/146381-12186astor_kontrolery_pacsystemsrx3i.jpg)

### PACSystems RX3i controllers
are solutions designed for the automation of medium and large-scale control systems. These systems not only handle a large number of I/O signals but also demand extensive functionality, rich communication capabilities, and high reliability.


The advantage of the RX3i system over other solutions in the market is primarily its flexible, open, and fully scalable architecture. Powerful central processing units enable rapid execution of algorithms for discrete, analog, and motion control. One significant benefit of this series is the mounting racks equipped with two buses, allowing the installation of modules dedicated to RX3i as well as modules from the 90-30 controllers. This facilitates migration from older solutions to modern systems, utilizing existing module inventory and significantly reducing costs.


Using the PACSystems RX3i series, it's possible to build redundant, high-availability systems with seamless control switching for applications where unplanned downtime results in significant financial losses, poses risks to human life and health, or threatens the natural environment.


The RX3i system comes with the most powerful processor among systems in its class available on the market, resulting in high data processing speed and improved system performance. Programming, servicing, and system expansion can be carried out on the fly using the Test Mode function, which allows for quick reversion to the last stable version of the program if any introduced changes cause instability.


### Programming with Test Mode
PACSystems RX3i controllers can be programmed using LD, ST, C, and FBD languages. There is a range of ready-made functions available to support application development, including program testing on-the-fly, symbolic variables, parameterized function blocks, and indirect addressing. RX3i also allows for on-the-fly programming. In addition to these programming capabilities, access to the PPS function block library is available, enabling the use of PACSystems RX3i in Hybrid DCS systems.

### Uninterrupted Operation
As mentioned above,Programming of PACSystems RX3i
takes place without stopping the controller,
with the added capability of testing introduced changes. This mechanism allows for
quick rollback of made corrections
in the program in case of poorly written applications. An additional convenience for system operators is the ability to exchange input/output modules while the
controller is in operation.

### PACSystems RX3i other features include:


▪ Fast Pentium III processors up to 1 GHz and patented high-efficiency, collision-free data exchange technology.

▪ Two buses in each baseplate slot for handling RX3i and 90-30 modules.

▪ Up to 64 MB of RAM and FLASH memory for control program storage and controller documentation (formats like Word, Excel, PDF, CAD, etc.).

▪ Communication flexibility through support for Ethernet, Genius, Profibus, DeviceNet, ProfiNet modules, as well as serial modules with support for protocols like Modbus RTU, DNP3.0, ASCII, SNPX.

▪ A wide range of available modules, including discrete inputs/outputs, universal analog inputs/outputs, isolated analog inputs/outputs, high-resolution analog inputs/outputs, fast counters (HSC), and motor control modules.

▪ Hardware interrupt support.

▪ Hot-swappable input/output module replacement 

▪ Local and remote expansion capabilities.

▪ Suitable for high-availability systems, continuous and batch applications, as well as motion control applications.




Everywhere where a control device needs to provide:
fast processing of large amounts of data, communication with multiple devices in various
protocols, control of servo drive systems, easy creation of advanced
applications, servicing during operation, uninterrupted operation, and much more, it's worth considering
the use of PACSystem RX3i.

## Astraada One Compact

![image](https://github.com/mjankowski22/programmable-logic-controllers---ebook-jwszolek/assets/100532969/78f58378-a237-4709-bc79-1b7db03c03d6)

Astraada One represents a series of advanced controllers that offer expanded functionalities beyond what standard PLCs can provide. These controllers are particularly well-suited for deployment in smaller to medium-sized control setups. Let's look into the key attributes of Astraada One Compact:

**1. Compact Design with Input/Output Expansion:**
   - Despite their compact size, Astraada One Compact controllers enable the augmentation of input and output capabilities through purpose-built expansion modules. These modules are capable of handling both digital and analog signals, allowing for a versatile configuration of the control system.

**2. Communication via the EtherCAT Protocol:**
   - Data exchange with expansion modules is facilitated by the EtherCAT protocol. This advanced communication protocol ensures swift and dependable data transmission among devices.
   
**3. Visualization via a Web Browser:**
   - Astraada One controllers boast networking capabilities and feature a web-based interface. This enables the configuration of controllers via a web browser and the creation of visualizations accessible on various   devices, including smartphones, computers, and SMART TVs.
   
**4. High Performance:**
   - Thanks to a processor operating at a clock frequency of 0.8 GHz, Astraada One Compact controllers deliver high-performance processing of control signals. They can adeptly manage multiple active communication protocols concurrently, a pivotal capability in advanced control systems.
   
**5. Extensive Communication Capabilities:**
   - Astraada One controllers support a multitude of communication protocols, offering flexibility in integration with diverse devices and systems. Supported protocols encompass Modbus RTU/TCP-IP, Profinet, CANopen, and EtherCAT.
   
**6. Open Programming Environment Codesys:**
   - Astraada One leverages the open programming environment known as Codesys, renowned for its popularity and flexibility. This translates to straightforward and intuitive controller programming, with access to numerous libraries and databases that simplify the creation of even intricate control programs.

**7. Some in-built features:**
   - **Digital Inputs**:
16 discrete inputs, meaning it can receive 16 different binary signals
   - **Counters:**
The ability to activate 2 fast counters that can operate at a frequency of 500 kHz. These counters can be used to measure fast events or pulses.
   - **Digital Outputs:**
16 discrete outputs, each of which can control a load with a current of up to 0.5 A. These outputs can be used to control various devices or components.
   - **Analog Outputs:**
6 analog outputs, each of which generates a signal in the range of 0V to 10V.
   - **Analog Inputs:**
12 analog inputs, of which 6 can handle signals ranging from -10V to +10V or from 0mA to 20mA, or PT100/PT1000 two-wire sensors. The remaining 6 inputs can handle signals ranging from -10V to +10V or from 0mA to 20mA, or they can handle three-wire return signals.
